Sustainable Energy

Scientists and Environmentalists have long been pushing the adoption of renewables as alternatives to our current energy sources. But what are our current energy sources? What is their impact on the environment? How far have countries come on their way to renewables adoption?

Let's keep all the scientific mumbo-jumbo aside and understand for real where our energy comes from, it's impact on our environment, and sustainable alternatives.
